## Description

Volume&Power is an NVDA add-on that provides quick control over your computer's volume and power functions.  
It allows you to adjust NVDA's speech volume and perform system shutdown or restart directly from the keyboard.

## Hotkeys

| Shortcut                  | Action                     |
|---------------------------|----------------------------|
| `NVDA+Shift+PageUp`       | Increase NVDA volume       |
| `NVDA+Shift+PageDown`     | Decrease NVDA volume       |
| `Alt+Windows+R`           | Restart computer           |
| `Alt+Windows+S`           | Shutdown computer          |

## Function Details

### Volume Control

- **Increase Volume**  
  Press `NVDA+Shift+PageUp` to increase NVDA's speech volume by 5%.  
  NVDA will announce the new volume percentage.

- **Decrease Volume**  
  Press `NVDA+Shift+PageDown` to decrease NVDA's speech volume by 5%.  
  NVDA will announce the new volume percentage.

### Power Control

- **Restart Computer**  
  Press `Alt+Windows+R` to restart your computer.  
  You will hear a confirmation sound and message, then the system will restart after 3 seconds.

- **Shutdown Computer**  
  Press `Alt+Windows+S` to shut down your computer.  
  You will hear a confirmation sound and message, then the system will power off after 3 seconds.

## Important Notes

> - Volume adjustments are saved and will persist between NVDA restarts.  
> - Power functions (restart/shutdown) give you a **3-second delay** to cancel if needed  
>   (by pressing Ctrl+Alt+Del or other system commands).
